APJ ABDUL KALAM

1931-2015

Biography

        

     Born: 15 October 1931, Rameswaram
    Died: 27 July 2015 (age 83 years), Shillong
 Awards: Bharat Ratna, Veer Savarkar Award, Padma Bhushan
    

The Missile Man of India: Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam
His full name was Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam,
born in Rameswaram of Madras Presidency
on 15th October 1931 and died on July 27, 2015, Shillong. 
He was an Indian Scientist and also a politician leader,
who later became the 11th President of India.
